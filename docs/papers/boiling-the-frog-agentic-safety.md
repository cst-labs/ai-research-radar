## Takeaway

Agentic safety evaluation needs stateful multi-turn scenarios that score what agents do, not only what they say.

## Summary

Introduces a multi-turn benchmark for tool-using AI agents in corporate and office settings, testing whether incremental attacks can lead to unsafe workspace states.
